How It Works

Simple. Fast. Life-Saving.

Three easy steps to connect you with the blood you need, or help you donate to save a life.

1
📝

Register / Post Request

Donors register with their blood group and location. Seekers post emergency requests with hospital details and urgency level.

2
🔗

Smart Matching

Our algorithm instantly matches seekers with the most compatible, available donors and nearest blood banks in real-time.

3
❤️

Connect & Save
